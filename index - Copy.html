About Me

I am a Android Developer

Hi there everyone this is Ruhul Amin. I’m an Android Developer, passionate about building great apps. For me app development is not just about coding, it’s also about creating an awesome user experience.

So far, I have worked on almost 8+ apps. Most of these apps were developed while I was studing at diploma in engineering . In my portfolio, I am only showcasing apps that I have personally designed and developed. I have done Diploma in Computer Engineering from Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute.

  • I have created more than 5 Android apps on Play store. In this process, I realized that well-written code always comes back to help you.
  • I cannot stress the importance of efficient, scalable and reusable code.
  • It saved a lot of my and my team’s time.
  • Writing good code comes out of practice, experience and lots of code reading.
  • A good UI/UX design is also the key to your app’s success.
  • Design should be geared towards making the app easy to use for any random user.
